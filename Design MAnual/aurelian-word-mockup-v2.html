2.2 Commissioning Process

Each equipment cell follows a structured four-stage qualification protocol before entering production service:

  1. 1
    Factory Acceptance Test (FAT)
    Vendor-site verification of mechanical tolerances, spindle run-out and control-system firmware against purchase specifications.
  2. 2
    Site Acceptance Test (SAT)
    On-site installation validation including power-quality checks, coolant integration and MES handshake confirmation.
  3. 3
    First Article Inspection (FAI)
    Production of representative defence-grade components with full CMM dimensional reporting against GD&T datums.
  4. 4
    Production Release
    Final sign-off by Quality Engineering; cell enters the MES scheduling pool and is available for customer orders.

4. Next Steps

The following activities are prioritised for Q2 2026 to complete the transition from commissioning to full-rate production:

  • Complete Wire EDM (Cell D) site acceptance test and integrate into production scheduling by end of March
  • Commission robotic load/unload system (FANUC) for lights-out operation of Cells A and B
  • Finalise AS9100D Stage 2 audit preparation and submit documentation package to certification body
  • Onboard two additional CNC programmers to support second-shift operations from April
  • Deliver first customer shipment under LOI-2025-017 (titanium airframe brackets) by mid-May
  • Deploy real-time SPC dashboard integration between CMM and MES for automated quality gating
