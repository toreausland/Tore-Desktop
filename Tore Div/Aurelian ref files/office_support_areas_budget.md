## Summary
The total investment for office spaces and support areas within the 5,000 square meter manufacturing facility is estimated at 48,600,000 NOK, with annual operating expenses of approximately 5,700,000 NOK. This budget provides for high-quality, functional spaces that support the manufacturing operations while creating an attractive work environment for staff and impressive facilities for visitors and clients.

The office spaces are designed to integrate seamlessly with the manufacturing areas, particularly on the main floor where design, engineering, and manufacturing support functions are located adjacent to the production areas for maximum efficiency and collaboration.
