# Comprehensive Investment Budget for Fortress Manufacturing

## Executive Summary

This document presents the comprehensive investment budget for the Fortress Manufacturing facility in Våler in Østfold, Norway. The facility will consist of a 5,000 square meter manufacturing floor with three specialized Production-On-Demand (POD) cells, plus 3,000 square meters of office space distributed between the main floor and upper floors.

The total investment required is estimated at **536,250,000 NOK** (approximately €48.75 million or $53.63 million), with a phased implementation approach over 30 months. This budget includes all equipment, infrastructure, building construction, office spaces, and implementation costs necessary to establish a state-of-the-art precision manufacturing facility capable of producing components for the offshore, aerospace, and defense industries.

## Return on Investment Analysis

### Key Financial Metrics
- Estimated annual revenue at full capacity: 350,000,000 NOK
- Estimated annual EBITDA: 105,000,000 NOK (30% margin)
- Simple payback period: 5.1 years
- IRR (10-year): 18.5%
- NPV (10-year, 8% discount rate): 320,000,000 NOK

### Sensitivity Analysis
- 10% increase in capital costs: Payback period extends to 5.6 years
- 10% decrease in revenue: Payback period extends to 5.7 years
- 10% increase in operating costs: Payback period extends to 5.4 years

## Risk Assessment and Mitigation

### Key Investment Risks
- Equipment cost escalation: Mitigated through early procurement and fixed-price contracts
- Construction delays: Mitigated through experienced project management and contingency
- Market demand fluctuations: Mitigated through flexible POD approach and diverse customer base
- Technology obsolescence: Mitigated through modular design and upgrade pathways

### Contingency Allocation
- Equipment contingency: 6,250,000 NOK
- Building contingency: 8,000,000 NOK
- Systems integration contingency: 5,000,000 NOK
- Schedule and technical risk contingency: 10,000,000 NOK
- **Total Contingency**: 29,250,000 NOK (5.5% of total investment)

## Conclusion

The total investment of 536,250,000 NOK will establish Fortress Manufacturing as a state-of-the-art precision manufacturing facility capable of serving the offshore, aerospace, and defense industries with high-quality components. The phased implementation approach minimizes risk while enabling the earliest possible start of production.

The pre-seed funding requirement of 25,000,000 NOK will enable the critical first phase of the project, including detailed engineering, permitting, and initial procurement activities. This investment represents a significant opportunity to establish a competitive manufacturing capability in Norway, leveraging the strategic location of Våler in Østfold and the growing demand for precision components in European markets.
