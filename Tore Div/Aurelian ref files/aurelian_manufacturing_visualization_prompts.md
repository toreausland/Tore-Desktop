# Aurelian Manufacturing Visualization Prompts

## Brand Identity Elements

### Company Name
- Aurelian Manufacturing

### Brand Essence
- Precision, innovation, automation, and reliability
- Inspired by the Roman Emperor Aurelian who was known for rebuilding and strengthening the empire
- Represents the golden standard in precision manufacturing (Aurelian derives from Latin "aureus" meaning "golden")

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#0A2463) and metallic gold (#FFD700)
- Secondary: Brushed aluminum silver (#C0C0C0), precision white (#F8F8FF), and industrial gray (#36454F)

### Typography
- Primary font: Modern, clean sans-serif for technical precision
- Secondary font: Subtle serif font for heritage and reliability

### Visual Elements
- Geometric precision in all design elements
- Clean lines and sharp angles
- Subtle gold accents against industrial backgrounds
- High-contrast lighting to highlight precision components
