## Market Pricing Factors

### Premium Pricing Factors
- **Rapid Delivery**: 15-25% premium for expedited production
- **Complex Geometries**: 10-30% premium for difficult-to-machine features
- **Tight Tolerances**: 10-20% premium for precision beyond industry standards
- **Material Certification**: 5-15% premium for specialized material certifications
- **Low Volume Production**: 10-30% premium for small batch sizes

### Competitive Pricing Factors
- **Production Volume**: 5-15% discount for larger production runs
- **Long-term Contracts**: 5-10% discount for multi-year supply agreements
- **Standard Materials**: 5-10% discount for commonly used materials
- **Repeat Orders**: 3-8% discount for repeat production of identical components

## Revenue Risk Factors

### Market Risks
- **Aerospace Industry Cycles**: 10-15% potential revenue impact
- **Defense Budget Fluctuations**: 5-10% potential revenue impact

- **Price Competition**: 5-10% potential revenue impact

### Operational Risks
- **Production Delays**: 5-15% potential revenue impact
- **Quality Issues**: 10-20% potential revenue impact
- **Supply Chain Disruptions**: 5-10% potential revenue impact
- **Mitigation Strategy**: Robust quality systems and supplier qualification program

## Conclusion

The revenue projections for Fortress Manufacturing demonstrate strong potential across all scenarios, with full capacity annual revenue ranging from 1,082.8 MNOK in the low case to 1,451.4 MNOK in the best case. The medium case projection of 1,295.8 MNOK represents a realistic target for the facility once fully operational.

The revenue ramp-up timeline aligns with the production capacity analysis, showing gradual growth as each POD becomes operational and reaches full capacity. By Year 5, the facility is projected to achieve 85-100% of its full revenue potential, depending on the scenario.

These revenue projections provide a solid foundation for the comprehensive P&L analysis and will be used to develop detailed financial models for the Fortress Manufacturing business plan.
