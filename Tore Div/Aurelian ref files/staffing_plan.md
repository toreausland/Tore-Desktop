# Project Aurelian Staffing Plan
## From Kickoff to Pre-Seed Funding and Initial Implementation

This document outlines the comprehensive staffing plan for Project Aurelian, detailing the roles, responsibilities, and hiring timeline from initial kickoff through securing pre-seed funding and into early implementation.

## Founding Team Structure

The founding team will lead Project Aurelian from kickoff through completion of the pre-seed funding round and initial implementation planning. This core team consists of four key positions:

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- **Responsibilities**: Overall leadership, investor relations, strategic direction, business development
- **Required Background**: Manufacturing industry experience, startup leadership, fundraising experience
- **Timeline**: From project kickoff (Month 0)

### Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- **Responsibilities**: Manufacturing technology strategy, equipment selection, digital twin implementation, technical partnerships
- **Required Background**: Advanced manufacturing expertise, CNC technology experience, digital manufacturing systems
- **Timeline**: From project kickoff (Month 0)

### Chief Operations Officer (COO)
- **Responsibilities**: Facility planning, production system design, supply chain development, operational strategy
- **Required Background**: Manufacturing operations, facility planning, production system implementation
- **Timeline**: From project kickoff (Month 0)

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
- **Responsibilities**: Financial planning, capital strategy, investor materials, funding coordination
- **Required Background**: Manufacturing finance, startup funding, capital planning
- **Timeline**: From project kickoff (Month 0)

## Detailed Role Descriptions for Key Positions

### Manufacturing Engineer
- **Responsibilities**: Facility layout design, production flow optimization, equipment specification
- **Required Skills**: Manufacturing engineering degree, facility design experience, CNC manufacturing knowledge
- **Reporting To**: CTO

### Digital Manufacturing Specialist
- **Responsibilities**: Digital twin implementation, manufacturing software integration, automation planning
- **Required Skills**: Digital manufacturing experience, simulation software expertise, Industry 4.0 knowledge
- **Reporting To**: CTO

### Quality Systems Manager
- **Responsibilities**: Quality system development, certification planning, inspection protocols
- **Required Skills**: AS9100 experience, quality management systems, aerospace/defense quality background
- **Reporting To**: COO

### Supply Chain Manager
- **Responsibilities**: Supplier identification, relationship management, procurement strategy
- **Required Skills**: Manufacturing supply chain experience, international sourcing, contract negotiation
- **Reporting To**: COO

### CNC Programming Lead
- **Responsibilities**: CAM system implementation, programming standards, tooling strategies
- **Required Skills**: Advanced CNC programming experience, 5-axis machining expertise, CAM software mastery
- **Reporting To**: CTO

### Project Manager
- **Responsibilities**: Implementation coordination, timeline management, contractor oversight
- **Required Skills**: Manufacturing project management, facility implementation experience, stakeholder management
- **Reporting To**: COO

## Recruitment Strategy

### Founding Team Assembly
- Leverage industry networks to identify experienced manufacturing leaders
- Focus on complementary skill sets across the founding team
- Emphasize equity-based compensation to align interests with company success

### Early Hires (Pre-Funding)
- Target experienced professionals willing to join an early-stage venture
- Offer competitive compensation with equity components
- Focus on versatile individuals who can handle multiple responsibilities

### Post-Funding Expansion
- Develop relationships with technical schools and universities for operator pipeline
- Engage specialized recruitment firms for technical positions
- Implement structured onboarding and training programs
- Create clear career progression paths to attract talent

## Training and Development

### Initial Training Approach
- Founding team to develop core training materials
- Partner with equipment suppliers for specialized training
- Develop digital twin-based training simulations
- Create standard operating procedures for all processes

### Ongoing Development
- Cross-training program to ensure operational flexibility
- Regular skill assessment and development planning
- Technical certification support for key personnel
- Leadership development for high-potential employees

## Conclusion

This staffing plan provides a comprehensive roadmap for building the Project Aurelian team from initial concept through pre-seed funding and into early implementation. The plan emphasizes a lean approach during the early phases, with the founding team handling multiple responsibilities, and strategic expansion as the project progresses and funding is secured.

The phased hiring approach aligns with the project timeline and capital requirements, ensuring that the right talent is in place at the right time to support each stage of development. By focusing on key technical and leadership roles early, the plan establishes a strong foundation for building a world-class manufacturing organization capable of delivering high-precision components for offshore, airforce, and military applications in Norway and beyond.
