## PROFILE

Fredrik Vereide Vangsal is a technology leader and entrepreneur with deep expertise in IoT, AI-based sensor analysis, cloud data infrastructure and systems architecture. As CEO of Veira AS, he has led complex technology projects for public-sector clients including the Norwegian Public Roads Administration (Statens vegvesen), Norwegian Customs (Tolletaten), Nye Veier and Moss Municipality — delivering scalable, secure and data-driven solutions for traffic analysis, mobility intelligence and border monitoring.

At Aurelian Manufacturing, Fredrik serves as Co-Founder and Technical Advisor with responsibility for automation architecture, data infrastructure strategy and digital thread design. His experience building production-grade AI and sensor systems — including computer vision, sensor fusion and cloud-based data pipelines — translates directly to Aurelian's challenge of designing autonomous CNC manufacturing systems with real-time monitoring, quality assurance and predictive analytics.

Fredrik combines strong technical depth in software architecture, API development and data platform design with proven project leadership and client management skills honed through years of delivering mission-critical systems for demanding government clients. His entrepreneurial track record and hands-on technical expertise make him a natural fit for building Aurelian's digital backbone from greenfield.
