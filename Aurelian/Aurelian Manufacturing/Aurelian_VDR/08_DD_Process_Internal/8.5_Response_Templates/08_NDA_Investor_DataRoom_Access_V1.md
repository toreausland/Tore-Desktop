## 1. Purpose

The Company is considering a private financing transaction and is prepared to disclose certain confidential information to the Recipient for the sole purpose of evaluating a potential investment in the Company (the "Purpose").

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("NDA") governs the terms under which confidential information is disclosed and the Recipient is granted access to the Company's Virtual Data Room ("VDR").

---

## 2. Confidential Information

**2.1 Definition:** "Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by the Company to the Recipient, whether in writing, orally, electronically, or by inspection, including but not limited to:

- Business plans, financial models, projections, and forecasts
- Revenue, cost, and profitability data
- Customer information, contracts, and pipeline
- Technology descriptions, equipment specifications, and trade secrets
- Cap table, shareholder structure, and valuation information
- Strategic plans, market analysis, and competitive intelligence
- Team information, compensation, and organizational plans
- Legal documents, governance materials, and IP portfolio
- Any document accessed through the Company's Virtual Data Room

**2.2 Exclusions:** Confidential Information does not include information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient
- Was already in the Recipient's possession before disclosure, as evidenced by written records
- Is independently developed by the Recipient without use of Confidential Information
- Is disclosed to the Recipient by a third party not under confidentiality obligations to the Company

---

## 3. Obligations of the Recipient

**3.1** The Recipient shall:

- Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the Company's prior written consent
- Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care as the Recipient uses for its own confidential information, and in no event less than reasonable care
- Limit access to Confidential Information to those of its employees, officers, directors, partners, advisors, and consultants ("Representatives") who have a need to know for the Purpose and who are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those in this NDA

**3.2** The Recipient shall be responsible for any breach of this NDA by its Representatives.

**3.3** The Recipient shall not:

- Copy, reproduce, or distribute Confidential Information except as reasonably necessary for the Purpose
- Use Confidential Information to compete with the Company or for any purpose other than the Purpose
- Reverse-engineer, decompile, or analyze any technology or know-how disclosed
- Contact the Company's customers, suppliers, employees, or business partners based on information obtained from the VDR, without the Company's prior written consent

## 4. Data Room Access Authorization

### 4.1 Investment Round Access

The Recipient is authorized to access the Company's Virtual Data Room documents relevant to the following investment round(s):

**Please check the applicable round(s):**

- [ ] **PreSeed Round** — Formation documents, founding team, initial business plan, PreSeed valuation basis
- [ ] **Seed Round** — Full financial model, economic projections, market analysis, competitive landscape, technical specifications, production timeline, quality roadmap, risk register, use of funds, sensitivity analysis, customer pipeline, GTM strategy

Access is limited to the documents relevant to the selected round(s). The Company reserves the right to restrict, expand, or revoke access at its sole discretion.

The Recipient acknowledges that the Company may track and log VDR access, including timestamps, documents viewed, and download activity. This data is used for process management purposes only.

## 5. No Obligation

**5.1** This NDA does not obligate either party to enter into any transaction or further agreement.

**5.2** The Company makes no representation or warranty as to the accuracy or completeness of any Confidential Information. The Recipient acknowledges that projections and forecasts are forward-looking statements based on assumptions and are not guarantees of future performance.

**5.3** The Company reserves the right to modify the terms of any proposed transaction or to terminate discussions at any time without liability.

---

## 6. Return or Destruction

**6.1** Upon the Company's written request, or upon termination of discussions, the Recipient shall promptly:

- Return all physical copies of Confidential Information
- Permanently delete all electronic copies, including from backup systems, to the extent reasonably practicable
- Confirm destruction in writing within 10 business days

**6.2** Notwithstanding the above, the Recipient may retain one archival copy solely for legal compliance purposes, subject to continued confidentiality obligations.

---

## 7. Term

**7.1** This NDA is effective from the date of execution and remains in effect for **3 years** from the date of last disclosure of Confidential Information.

**7.2** The confidentiality obligations shall survive expiration or termination of this NDA for the full term specified above.
