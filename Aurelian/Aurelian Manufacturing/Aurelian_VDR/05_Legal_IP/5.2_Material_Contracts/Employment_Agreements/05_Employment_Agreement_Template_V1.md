## 4. Equity and Vesting

**4.1** The Employee's equity interest in the Company is held through [holding company name and org.nr.], as set out in the Shareholders Agreement (VDR 01.02).

## 5. Intellectual Property

**5.1** All intellectual property created by the Employee in connection with the Company's business — including inventions, designs, software, trade secrets, know-how, and other proprietary rights — shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

**5.2** The Employee has executed an IP Assignment Declaration dated 11 February 2026, as referenced in the Shareholders Agreement §13 and VDR 05.01.

**5.3** The Employee shall promptly disclose all inventions and IP to the Company and execute such documents as necessary to perfect the Company's ownership.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

**6.1** The Employee shall maintain strict confidentiality regarding all non-public information relating to the Company's business, technology, customers, suppliers, financial affairs, and strategic plans.

**6.2** This obligation survives termination of employment for a period of **5 years**, consistent with the Shareholders Agreement §10.

**6.3** Breach of confidentiality entails liability for damages and a contractual penalty of NOK 200,000 per breach, as defined in the Shareholders Agreement §10.

---

## 7. Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation

**7.1 Non-Competition:** For a period of **2 years** following termination of employment, the Employee shall not, directly or indirectly, engage in, own, manage, operate, or participate in any business that competes with the Company's business within Norway or within 100 km of any Company facility, consistent with the Shareholders Agreement §9.

**7.2 Non-Solicitation:** For a period of **2 years** following termination of employment, the Employee shall not solicit or recruit any employee, advisor, or consultant of the Company, or solicit business from any customer or prospective customer of the Company.

**7.3 Compensation:** During any post-termination non-competition period, the Company shall pay compensation in accordance with the Norwegian Working Environment Act §14A.

**7.4 Contractual Penalty:** Breach of non-competition obligations entails a contractual penalty of NOK 2,000,000 per breach, as defined in the Shareholders Agreement §9, in addition to any damages.

---

## 8. Termination

**8.1 Notice Period:** Either party may terminate this agreement by giving **[3 / 6] months** written notice. The notice period commences on the first day of the month following receipt of notice.

**8.2 Summary Dismissal:** The Company may terminate the employment with immediate effect in accordance with the Norwegian Working Environment Act §15-14 (avskjed) in cases of gross breach of duty or other material default.

**8.3 Garden Leave:** During the notice period, the Company may place the Employee on garden leave (fritak for arbeidsplikt). The Employee shall remain available for consultation and shall not commence employment with any third party during the notice period.

**8.4 Return of Property:** Upon termination, the Employee shall immediately return all Company property, documents, data, and equipment. Access to Company systems shall be revoked upon the effective date of termination.

---

## 9. Governing Law and Disputes

**9.1** This agreement is governed by Norwegian law.

**9.2** Any dispute arising from or in connection with this agreement shall be resolved by arbitration under the rules of the Oslo Chamber of Commerce, consistent with the Shareholders Agreement §14.

**9.3** The parties shall first attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation.
