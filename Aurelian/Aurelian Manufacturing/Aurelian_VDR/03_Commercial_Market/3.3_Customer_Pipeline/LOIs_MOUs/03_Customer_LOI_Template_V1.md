## 1. Purpose

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") expresses the mutual interest of the parties in establishing a commercial relationship whereby Aurelian provides precision CNC manufacturing services to the Customer.

This LOI is non-binding and does not create any obligation on either party to enter into a definitive supply agreement. It is intended to document the parties' mutual interest and provide a basis for further discussion and qualification.

---

## 2. Aurelian Manufacturing — Overview

Aurelian Manufacturing is establishing a purpose-built autonomous CNC manufacturing facility in Valer, Ostfold, Norway, designed for high-mix, low-volume (HMLV) precision machining. The facility is configured for:

- Multi-axis CNC machining (3-axis through 5-axis simultaneous)
- Multi-tasking turn/mill operations
- Additive manufacturing, milling /turning multi service, 3D metal printing services.
- Complex geometries in metals including steel, aluminum, titanium, and specialty alloys
- Progressive automation enabling competitive lead times and consistent quality
- Digital thread from order to delivery — full traceability and documentation

**Target production start:** Q3 2027

**Quality certifications planned:**
- ISO 9001 (target Q4 2027)
- AS9100 — Aerospace quality management (target Q3-Q4 2028)
- AQAP 2110 — NATO quality assurance (target Q3-Q4 2028)

## 4. Pricing Framework

**4.1** Aurelian's manufacturing services are priced on a per-part basis, derived from estimated CNC machine hours, material costs, and setup/programming time.

**4.2** Detailed quotations will be provided on a part-by-part basis upon receipt of technical drawings and specifications.

**4.3** For customers with significant annual volume (indicatively exceeding [X] CNC hours per year), Aurelian offers a partnership pricing model with shared benefit structures for sustained capacity utilization.
