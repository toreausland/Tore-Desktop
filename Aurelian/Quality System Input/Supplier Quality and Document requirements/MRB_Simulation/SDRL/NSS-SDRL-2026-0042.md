## Purpose

The SDRL is the customer's master specification defining all documentation requirements for the order. It specifies which documents the supplier must produce, their submission timing, review requirements, and whether they must be included in the MRB or shipment documentation.

## Document Type Categories (Authoritative Definition)

These 8 categories are the standard classification for ALL technical documentation:

```
1. GENERAL        — Administrative & project management
2. TECHNICAL       — Bid documentation & technical submittals
3. ENGINEERING     — Design & engineering outputs
4. DISPATCH        — Logistics, packing, preservation
5. USER_MANUAL     — Operation & maintenance documentation
6. CERTIFICATES    — Manufacturing & verification certificates
7. PROCEDURES      — Manufacturing & verification procedures
8. REPORTS         — Manufacturing & verification reports
```
