## Purpose

Records results of visual inspection (VT) performed on machined components. Documents surface condition, dimensional conformance at a visual level, and workmanship quality.

## Content

- Visual inspection records per component
- Surface condition assessment
- Workmanship evaluation
- Acceptance criteria per applicable standards
- Inspector identification and qualifications
- Reference to ISO 17637 (general principles for surface inspection)

## Key Standards

- ISO 17637 (Visual testing — general principles applicable to surface inspection)

## Notes

- Generalised from weld-specific inspection to component surface inspection
- ISO 17637 description updated to remove fusion weld reference
