CERTIFICATE OF CONFORMANCE

Aurelian Manufacturing AS hereby certifies that the following equipment has been manufactured, inspected, and tested in accordance with the requirements of Purchase Order NSS-PO-2026-0042 and all referenced specifications, codes, and standards.

Compliance Statements

  1. All materials comply with NORSOK M-001 and ASTM A182 F53, with material certificates per EN 10204 Type 3.2.
  2. All welding has been performed in accordance with qualified welding procedures (ASME IX / EN ISO 15614) by qualified welders (EN ISO 9606).
  3. Non-destructive examination (VT, RT, PT, PMI) performed per applicable codes with all results meeting acceptance criteria.
  4. Hydrostatic pressure test performed at 517 barg with 30-minute hold. No leaks detected. Pressure drop 0.12% (acceptance <1%).
  5. Dimensional inspection confirmed all critical dimensions within specified tolerances.
  6. Surface treatment applied per NSS-SPEC-CT-001 Rev 2. All DFT measurements within specification.
  7. Two non-conformance reports were raised during manufacturing (AM-NCR-2026-0042-001, -002). Both have been dispositioned and closed with NSS approval.
  8. All documentation compiled in Manufacturing Record Book AM-MRB-2026-0042-001.
