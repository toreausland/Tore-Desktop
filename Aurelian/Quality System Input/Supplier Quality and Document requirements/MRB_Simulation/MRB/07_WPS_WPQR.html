Additional Requirements

NORSOK M-601 Supplementary Requirements:
1. Back purge shall be maintained until a minimum of three (3) complete layers have been deposited.
2. Weld metal ferrite content shall be verified by metallographic examination on production test coupons.
3. No post-weld heat treatment (PWHT) is permitted for Super Duplex stainless steels.
4. All tack welds shall be made using qualified WPS and shall be fully re-fused into the root pass.
5. Interpass cleaning by stainless steel wire brush or grinding only. No carbon steel tooling permitted.
