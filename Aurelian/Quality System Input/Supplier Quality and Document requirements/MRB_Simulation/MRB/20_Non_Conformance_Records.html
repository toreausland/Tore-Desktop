Affected Item
Main Body Forging — Heat H24-5587

Description of Non-Conformance

During incoming PMI inspection of main body forging (Heat H24-5587), one test point (location 5 of 6) showed Cr content of 23.8%, slightly below the 24.0% minimum per ASTM A182 F53. Remaining 5 points all within specification (24.9–25.3%).

Root Cause Analysis

Segregation at surface of forging. Mill certificate shows 25.1% Cr on ladle analysis. Localized surface depletion suspected.

Corrective Action

Additional PMI measurements taken on 8 supplementary points around location 5. All readings 24.2–25.0% Cr. Original reading attributed to surface oxide. Surface ground 0.5mm and re-tested: 24.6% Cr. Material accepted for use.

Disposition

USE AS-IS
Justification: All supplementary PMI readings confirm Cr content within ASTM A182 F53 requirements. Original anomaly attributed to surface oxide contamination, confirmed by re-test after 0.5mm surface grinding (24.6% Cr). Ladle analysis on mill certificate (25.1% Cr) further supports material compliance. Material is fit for intended service.

Affected Item
Weld W-004 — Lifting Lug Fillet Weld

Description of Non-Conformance

During welding of W-004 (lifting lug fillet weld), tungsten inclusion detected by welder. Welding stopped immediately.

Root Cause Analysis

Momentary contact between tungsten electrode and weld pool due to restricted access in welding position.

Corrective Action

Affected area (approx. 25mm length) ground out completely. Area re-examined by PT — no indications. Re-welded per WPS AM-WPS-SD-002. Post-weld VT and PT performed — ACCEPTED.

Disposition

REWORK COMPLETED
Rework Summary: Defective weld area ground out, re-inspected by PT (clear), re-welded per approved WPS AM-WPS-SD-002, and post-weld VT and PT performed with acceptable results. Weld integrity confirmed.
