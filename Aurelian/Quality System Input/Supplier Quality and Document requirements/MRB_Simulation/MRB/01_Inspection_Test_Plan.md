## Purpose

The Inspection & Test Plan defines all inspection and test activities required during manufacturing of the Subsea Connector Assembly SCA-300. It specifies hold points, witness points, and review points for Supplier QC, Customer (NSS), and Third Party inspectors.

## Content

- Project reference data (PO, client, material, product)
- Applicable codes and standards (NORSOK M-001, M-630, M-650, M-501, ISO 9001:2015, EN 10204)
- ITP matrix with 17 inspection activities (Items 10–170)
- Inspection types: H (Hold), W (Witness), R (Review), M (Monitor)
- Records/certificates column referencing MRB sections
- Signature block for QC Manager, Project Manager, and Client QA

## Key Standards

- NORSOK M-001 (Materials selection)
- NORSOK M-630 (Material data sheets — duplex stainless steel)
- NORSOK M-650 (Qualification of special processes)
- NORSOK M-501 (Surface preparation and protective coating)
- ISO 9001:2015 (Quality management system)
- EN 10204 Type 3.2 (Metallic products — inspection documents)
