## Purpose

The Mechanical Completion Dossier is the final compilation document confirming that all manufacturing, inspection, and testing activities are complete and that the product is ready for dispatch.

## Content

- Mechanical completion checklist
- Punch list status (all items closed)
- Summary of all MRB sections with completion status
- Outstanding items register (should be zero at final issue)
- Sign-off by Project Manager, Quality Manager, and Client representative
- Release for shipment confirmation
