## Purpose

Contains material inspection certificates per EN 10204 Type 3.2 for all materials used in the manufacturing of the Subsea Connector Assembly SCA-300.

## Content

- EN 10204 Type 3.2 certificates (third-party validated)
- Chemical composition analysis
- Mechanical properties (tensile, yield, elongation, hardness)
- Heat treatment records
- Material specification compliance (UNS S32750 / SAF 2507)
- PMI results cross-referenced to material traceability

## Key Standards

- EN 10204 Type 3.2 (Inspection documents — specific inspection)
- NORSOK M-630 (Material data sheets for duplex stainless steel)
