## Purpose

Official weight certificate for the completed assembly. Required for subsea installation planning and load calculations.

## Content

- Component/assembly identification
- Dry weight measurement
- Weighing equipment details (calibration status)
- Comparison to design weight
- Measurement uncertainty
- Signed by authorised personnel
