## Purpose

The Certificate of Conformance is the supplier's formal declaration that the delivered product has been manufactured, inspected, and tested in accordance with the Purchase Order requirements and applicable specifications.

## Content

- PO reference and product identification
- Declaration of compliance to specifications
- List of applicable standards and codes
- Summary of inspections and tests performed
- Reference to MRB for supporting documentation
- Signed by Quality Manager and authorised representative

## Notes

- Final document issued — signifies manufacturing completion
- No pressure testing declaration (not applicable for CNC scope)
