## Purpose

Provides full material traceability from raw material receipt through manufacturing to final product. Traces heat numbers, certificate references, and material allocations to each component.

## Content

- Material specification (UNS S32750 / SAF 2507 Super Duplex)
- Heat number tracking
- Mill certificate cross-references (EN 10204 Type 3.2)
- Component-to-material mapping
- PMI verification records
