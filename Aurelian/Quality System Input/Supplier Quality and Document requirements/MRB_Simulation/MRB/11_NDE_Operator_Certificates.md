## Purpose

Contains copies of NDE operator qualification certificates for all personnel performing non-destructive testing on the order. Demonstrates competence per ISO 9712.

## Content

- Operator name, ID, and employer
- Certification level (Level I, II, or III)
- Certified methods (RT, PT, VT, PMI)
- Certification body and certificate numbers
- Validity dates
- Scope of certification

## Key Standards

- ISO 9712 (Non-destructive testing — Qualification and certification of NDT personnel)
