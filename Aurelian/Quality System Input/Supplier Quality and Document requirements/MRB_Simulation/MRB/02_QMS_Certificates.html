1. Purpose

This section of the Manufacturing Record Book (MRB) provides copies and summaries of the current quality management system certifications held by Aurelian Manufacturing AS. These certifications confirm the company's compliance with applicable international standards for quality, environmental, health & safety, and welding quality management.

All certificates listed below are current and valid as of the date of this document. Full certificate copies are attached to the physical MRB package.

4. Notes

  • Certificate Copies: Full copies of all certificates listed above are included as appendices to the physical MRB package. Digital copies are available in the Aurelian Manufacturing document management system (DMS).
  • Surveillance Audits: All certifications are subject to annual surveillance audits by DNV GL. The most recent surveillance audit was completed on 18 September 2025 with no major non-conformances identified.
  • ISO 3834-2 Renewal: The ISO 3834-2:2021 welding quality certification is scheduled for renewal audit in June 2026. Aurelian Manufacturing confirms the certification will remain active through the duration of this project.
  • Responsible Welding Coordinator (RWC): Mr. Thomas Bergstrøm, IWE (International Welding Engineer), serves as the designated Responsible Welding Coordinator per ISO 3834-2 requirements.
