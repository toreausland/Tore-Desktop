## Purpose

Records dimensional inspection results for all critical dimensions on the Subsea Connector Assembly SCA-300. Verifies that machined components meet drawing tolerances.

## Content

- Component identification and drawing reference
- Measured dimensions vs. nominal with tolerances
- CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) data where applicable
- Go/No-Go gauge results
- Acceptance disposition per dimension
- Inspector identification
