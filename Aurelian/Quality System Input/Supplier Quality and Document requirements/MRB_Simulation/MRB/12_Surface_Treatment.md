## Purpose

Documents the surface treatment (coating) inspection performed on manufactured components per NORSOK M-501 requirements.

## Content

- Surface preparation details (method, grade)
- Coating system applied
- Dry film thickness (DFT) measurements
- Environmental conditions during application
- Cure/flash-off times
- Inspection results and acceptance
- Reference to coating procedure

## Key Standards

- NORSOK M-501 (Surface preparation and protective coating)
