## Purpose

Individual Non-Destructive Examination reports for each NDE method applied during manufacturing. Covers Radiographic Testing (RT) and Penetrant Testing (PT) of machined components.

## Content

- RT reports with radiographic technique details
- PT reports with penetrant method parameters
- Component identification and test locations
- Acceptance criteria per applicable standards
- Results and disposition (Accept/Reject/Repair)
- NDE operator certification references

## Key Standards

- ISO 17636 (Radiographic testing)
- ISO 3452 (Penetrant testing)

## Notes

- Generalised from weld-specific NDE to component NDE
