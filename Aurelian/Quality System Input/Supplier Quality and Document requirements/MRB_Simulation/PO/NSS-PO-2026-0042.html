4. Quality Requirements

4.1 Quality Management System

The Supplier shall hold valid certification to ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 3834-2. Copies of certificates shall be included in the MRB (Section 2). The Supplier's QMS shall be available for audit by NSS upon request.

4.2 Inspection & Test Plan (ITP)

The Supplier shall submit an Inspection & Test Plan for NSS review and approval within 4 weeks after order (WAO). The ITP shall detail all inspection, testing, and hold/witness points. NSS reserves the right to witness any operation marked as Hold (H) or Witness (W) point.

4.3 Welding Requirements

  • All welding procedures (WPS) to be qualified per ASME IX or EN ISO 15614
  • All welders to be qualified per ASME IX or EN ISO 9606
  • PWHT per applicable code requirements
  • Weld log to track all production welds with full traceability
  • Ferrite content: 30–70% (measured per ASTM E562)

4.4 NDE Requirements

  • 100% visual inspection on all welds
  • 100% radiographic or ultrasonic examination on all pressure-containing welds
  • 100% liquid penetrant (PT) on all accessible surfaces
  • PMI on all CRA welds and base materials
  • NDE operators qualified to EN ISO 9712 Level 2 minimum

4.5 Pressure Testing

Hydrostatic pressure test shall be performed at 517 barg (1.5 × design pressure) with a minimum hold time of 30 minutes. Test medium: clean fresh water with inhibitor. All pressure test equipment shall have valid calibration certificates.

4.6 Dimensional Inspection

Full dimensional inspection per approved manufacturing drawings. Critical dimensions to be recorded on the dimensional inspection report. Dimensional inspection to be performed at ambient temperature (20 ± 5°C).

4.7 Surface Treatment

All external surfaces shall be prepared and coated per NSS Specification NSS-SPEC-CT-001 Rev 2. Minimum dry film thickness: 250 µm. Coating inspection report required.

4.8 Non-Conformance

All non-conformances shall be reported to NSS Quality immediately upon discovery. No concession or deviation from specification shall be accepted without prior written approval from NSS. NCR records shall be included in the MRB (Section 20).

5. HSE Requirements

The Supplier shall comply with all applicable Norwegian HSE legislation and NSS HSE requirements as detailed in NSS-HSE-REQ-001 Rev 3. Key requirements include:

  • Valid HSE management system certified to ISO 14001 and ISO 45001
  • Safe Job Analysis (SJA) for all critical operations
  • Incident reporting within 24 hours
  • NSS right of access for HSE audits
