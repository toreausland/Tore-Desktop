MRB Lifecycle

7-Stage Pipeline

From customer PO to archived MRB — every document tracked, validated, and compiled.

G
Pre-Production Quality Gate

Material Gate

PR-008 • aurelian-material-gate

AI-assisted incoming material review. 5-point certificate check against customer-specific requirement profiles. 3-way decision: RELEASED / REJECTED / REVIEW. Prevents non-conforming material from reaching the shop floor.

PR-008 material-gate IF-003 IF-004 IF-005
1

SDRL Intake & Parsing

PR-009 • aurelian-sdrl-parser

Customer SDRL extracted from PO. Parsed into Order Requirement Matrix. Industry classification (Defence 8-section / Oil & Gas 7-section). Data source routing assigned per document.

PR-009 sdrl-parser IF-001 IF-002 FM-009-01
2

MRB Index & Structure Initialization

PR-009 §6.6 • aurelian-mrb-builder

SDRL generates the order-specific MRB Index (variable per order). Folder structure created. Document slots populated. FAIR triggers evaluated (defence). ITP tracker initialized (Oil & Gas). The MRB Index becomes the live tracking document.

PR-009 mrb-builder FM-009-04 IF-006
3

Real-Time Document Collection

PR-009 §6.7 • aurelian-mrb-builder

Documents flow in from three routes: CNC/Metrology/Inspection (direct to MRB via MQTT/OPC-UA), Supplier docs (via ERP, one cert may serve multiple orders), Internal QA (process sheets, FAIR, NCRs). MRB Index updated as documents arrive.

mrb-builder IF-004 IF-006 IF-007
4

5-Layer Document Validation

PR-010 • aurelian-doc-validator

Every document passes 5 checks: L1 Existence → L2 Format → L3 Completeness → L4 Compliance → L5 Traceability. Failures generate Correction Requests. Cross-document traceability chain verified. MRB Index status updated per document.

PR-010 doc-validator FM-010-01 FM-010-03 IF-013 IF-014
5

Certificate of Conformance

PR-011 • aurelian-coc-generator

Prerequisite gate: all MRB documents must be VALIDATED before CoC generation. Industry-specific templates (Defence AS9100/AQAP, Oil & Gas NORSOK/API). Auto-populated from validated data. Deviations declared. Signed by authorized signatory.

PR-011 coc-generator FM-010-04 FM-011-01 IF-010
6

MRB Assembly & Release

PR-012 • aurelian-mrb-builder

MRB Index finalized (page numbers added). All validated documents compiled into single bookmarked PDF/A-3. Cover page, master index, section dividers. 10-point quality check. Release authorization. Electronic and/or physical delivery to customer.

PR-012 mrb-builder FM-012-02 IF-009 IF-011
7

Delivery & Archive

PR-013 • All skills

MRB shipped with product. Electronic delivery via customer portal. Active archive (2 years) → Deep archive. PDF/A with SHA-256 checksums. 15+ year retention (defence: 25–30 years). Annual integrity checks. Legal hold capability. Disaster recovery from encrypted off-site backups.
