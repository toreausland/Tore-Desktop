2.2 Commissioning Process

Each production unit follows a standardised four-step commissioning process before being cleared for customer work.

1
Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT)
Vendor-site validation of all mechanical, electrical, and software subsystems against purchase specifications.
2
Site Acceptance Testing (SAT)
On-premise verification of installation, utility connections, and integration with the facility MES and ERP systems.
3
First Article Inspection (FAI)
Production of a reference part with full dimensional inspection per AS9102 standard. Must achieve ≥92% Cpk on critical features.
4
Production Release
Formal sign-off by Quality and Operations. Equipment enters the production schedule for customer orders.
